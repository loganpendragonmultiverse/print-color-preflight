# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free community for everyone. Be constructive, discuss evidence and tradeoffs, and remember that print workflows vary by device, region, and production requirement.

## Expected behavior

- Communicate with patience and professional respect.
- Critique ideas and implementations rather than people.
- Share only files and profiles you have permission to distribute.
- State testing conditions and limitations honestly.
- Respect maintainers' scope and review decisions.

Harassment, discriminatory conduct, threats, deliberate disruption, and publication of private information are not acceptable. Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainer through the repository's security contact. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and restrict participation when necessary to protect the community.
